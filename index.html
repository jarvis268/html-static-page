Some intersting fact about moon

The brightest and largest object in our night sky, the Moon makes Earth a more livable planet by moderating our home planet's wobble on its axis, leading to a relatively stable climate. It also causes tides, creating a rhythm that has guided humans for thousands of years.

About The moon

  • Radius: 1,737.4 km
  • Orbital period: 27 days
  • Gravity: 1.62 m/s²
  • Distance to Earth: 384,400 km
  • Surface area: 3.793×107 km2; (0.074 of Earth's)
  • Surface pressure: 10−7 Pa (1 picobar) (day); 10−10 Pa (1 femtobar); (night);
  • Satellite of:Earth

Top 5 facts about moon

  1. The Moon is Earth’s only natural satellite.
  2. It takes 27.3 days for the Moon to travel all the way around the Earth.
  3. The Moon is a lot smaller than The Sun.
  4. The Moon was made when a rock smashed into Earth.
  5. The Moon controls the tides.
